Zinko Zoom

Zinko Zoom is a fast side-scroll runner inspired by classic arcade speed games. Race through colourful worlds, leap over hazards, and collect glowing DNA strands as the pace keeps building.

About the Game

Gameplay

Run, jump, collect DNA strands, avoid obstacles, and keep pushing forward as Zinko’s world becomes faster and more dangerous.

  • Side-scroll runner gameplay
  • DNA strand collection
  • Fast arcade feel inspired by Sonic-style movement
  • Powerups, hazards, hills, ledges, and increasing difficulty
  • Simple controls with skill-based mastery
Development Status

Current State

Zinko Zoom is currently under active development. Gameplay, visuals, progression, powerups, worlds, and balancing may continue to change as the game improves.
