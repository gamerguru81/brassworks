Where in the World is Zinko:
The Syndicate Files

A globe-trotting mystery adventure where every case is different.

Step into the shoes of Zinko, an up-and-coming investigator tracking strange crimes, stolen technologies, missing artifacts, and hidden conspiracies across the world. Follow clues, question suspects, solve puzzles, and travel between real-world inspired locations as you uncover a growing mystery hidden beneath seemingly ordinary cases.

No two investigations are the same. Crimes, suspects, clues, travel routes, and puzzle rooms are randomized each playthrough, creating a fresh detective experience every time you play.

Begin with lighthearted adventures and quirky mysteries, then rise through the ranks as the world of Zinko becomes darker, stranger, and far more dangerous than it first appears.

Investigate syndicate operations, uncover hidden organizations, and piece together a conspiracy stretching across the globe.
